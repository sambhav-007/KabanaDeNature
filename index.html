About Kabana de Nature

Kabana de Nature is a 5-star resort located in Rajgarh, Himachal Pradesh, known for offering a luxury getaway amidst serene pine forests. Situated in Village Kot, it is roughly a 3-hour drive from Chandigarh and provides a peaceful escape from urban noise. Guests enjoy curated dining, modern amenities, and spacious cottages designed for restful stays. Our thoughtfully designed rooms and warm hospitality create an atmosphere of calm, inviting you to unwind, explore the outdoors, and create unforgettable memories in harmony with nature.

Nature-led stay Wake up to pine-scented mornings and mountain air in a peaceful hilltop sanctuary.
Comfort-first design Modern rooms, cozy communal spaces, and attentive service create a refined yet relaxed escape.
